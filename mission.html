Our Mission

Our Mission is to expand AfriPower all over South Africa to empower
small businesses in Africa by providing them with a digital presence that is accessible to everyone.
We aim to collaborate with educational institutions and training programs to provide resources and learning opportunities to inspire young entrepreneurs.
We envision an energetic marketplace that connects users with unique, locally-made
products and services. We aim to be the go-to destination for those who appreciate the authenticity and craftsmanship of African entrepreneurs.
